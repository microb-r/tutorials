# Create BioSample on NCBI

BioSamples can be created [here](https://submit.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/subs/biosample/) and are required before submission of sequencing reads or genomic sequences.

1. Click on **New Submission**

2. Add/update information of submitter.

3. In **General Information**, select a specific release date (otherwise, it can be released immediately after submission).

<img width="710" alt="Screen Shot 2019-08-11 at 11 04 31 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48694025/63230015-4026f300-c1d5-11e9-83d8-ba14269d2227.png">

4. **Single BioSample:**

   4.1 Select **Single BioSample** and **Continue**.

   4.2 Select **Sample Type**. In the case of viruses, select **Virus Sample**.**Continue**.
  
  <img width="707" alt="Screen Shot 2019-08-11 at 11 04 43 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48694025/63230074-f985c880-c1d5-11e9-8535-99784a352a35.png">

   4.3 In **Attributes** section, fill in with corresponding information. Some issues with **Sample Name** can show up, confirm that the sample name in correct and **Continue** again.
  
  <img width="714" alt="Screen Shot 2019-08-11 at 11 11 06 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48694025/63230186-4e760e80-c1d7-11e9-90f0-cc90eb22f077.png">

    4.4 Add **BioProject** number (previously create a Bioproject [here](https://submit.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/subs/bioproject/)).

<img width="711" alt="Screen Shot 2019-08-11 at 11 05 52 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48694025/63230144-d4458a00-c1d6-11e9-9d2d-f07a4042db72.png">

    4.5 Accept suggested title on **Title and Comments** section or choose one on your own. **Continue**
  
  <img width="711" alt="Screen Shot 2019-08-11 at 11 06 07 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48694025/63230163-179ff880-c1d7-11e9-92df-6442e8619dac.png">
  
    4.6 Finally, review information and submit.
  
  <img width="709" alt="Screen Shot 2019-08-11 at 11 12 02 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48694025/63230176-343c3080-c1d7-11e9-88af-6034d8140b03.png">

5. **Batch Submission:**

    5.1 Select **Batch/Multiple BioSamples**.
  
  <img width="706" alt="Screen Shot 2019-08-11 at 11 12 59 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48694025/63230216-9f860280-c1d7-11e9-91a3-1a5ca5df1aac.png">
  
    5.2 Select **Sample Type**. In the case of viruses, select **Virus Sample**.**Continue**.
  
    5.3 In **Attributes** sction, select option **Upload a file using Excel or text format (tab-delimited) that includes the attributes for each of your BioSamples**. An example of how to fill this file can be found [here](https://github.com/microb-r/tutorials/blob/master/files/Viral_BioSample_Batch_Submission_Example.txt).
  
  <img width="708" alt="Screen Shot 2019-08-11 at 11 20 28 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48694025/63230236-e116ad80-c1d7-11e9-842f-d1db3a4a26cf.png">
  
    5.4 Load .txt file and Continue (ignore Warning about Sample Name and click **Continue** again).

    5.5 Finally, review information and submit.
